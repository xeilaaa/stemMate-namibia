A curriculum-aligned AI tutor built for Namibian learners.

StemMate helps Grade 10–12 learners in Mathematics, Physical Science, and Biology by searching approved Namibian textbooks, syllabuses, and past examination materials before generating answers — so support stays grounded in what learners are actually taught.

The Problem

Namibian learners are struggling in core STEM subjects.

2023 NSSC pass rates show persistent gaps — limiting progression into science, engineering, technology, and health careers that drive national development.

Schools face shortages of qualified STEM teachers, unequal access to support, and limited after-school help. Learners increasingly turn to general AI tools — but those tools were not designed for the Namibian curriculum.

The Solution

A tutor that checks the right textbook first.

Instead of answering from general internet knowledge, StemMate retrieves approved Namibian learning materials and generates responses grounded in trusted sources.

📚

Curriculum-aligned

Built on NSSC textbooks, syllabuses, and past papers — so answers reflect what learners actually need to know for national examinations.

🌍

Low-bandwidth ready

Designed to be useful in different school environments, including under-resourced and rural settings where connectivity is limited.

🤝

Extends teachers

Strengthens teacher reach rather than replacing them — providing after-hours revision support where private tutoring is out of reach.

How It Works

Retrieval-grounded learning in four steps.

1

Learner asks

A Grade 10–12 learner submits a question in Maths, Physical Science, or Biology.

2

System retrieves

StemMate searches approved NSSC textbooks, syllabi, and past exam materials first.

3

AI grounds answer

The AI generates a clear explanation anchored in the retrieved curriculum content.

4

Learner improves

The learner gets exam-relevant support, anywhere, anytime — even on low bandwidth.
